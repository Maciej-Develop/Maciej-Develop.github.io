### Bachelor of Computer Science (Application Development Orientation)

- **School:** [HE2B - ESI](https://he2b.be/formations/developpeur-dapplications/)
- **Location:** Brussels, Belgium
- **Period:** 09/2021 - 01/2026

### Description

This bachelor’s degree focuses on application development, offering a captivating and demanding curriculum oriented towards the creation, deployment, and maintenance of applications for the business world.

### Relevant Coursework

- Programming Languages: JAVA, C++
- Frameworks : Spring Boot, Laravel
- Data Structures and Algorithms
- Design Patterns
- Relational Databases (SQL)
- Agile Methodologies and Analysis (UML)
